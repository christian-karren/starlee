Starlee Capture

Starlee reads pages for local capture and sends captures only to your local Starlee app on 127.0.0.1. The desktop app owns onboarding, diagnostics, and your library; this extension owns page access. Your capture token is stored in this browser’s local extension storage and is never shown here after saving.

How capture works

Click the Starlee toolbar icon, the page button, or the Starlee Mac menu-bar item. The extension extracts readable text and metadata from the active tab, then posts it to the local Starlee service. Setup and capture-test status are shown here without page bodies, transcripts, selected text, cookies, or tokens.
